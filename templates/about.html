ABOUT US

Company Profile

DHK FMS commenced its operations in Chennai in 2020 and has since grown into a reliable provider of comprehensive manpower and facility management services across Tamil Nadu. We specialize in supplying skilled and unskilled manpower to support both commercial and domestic operations.

Our service portfolio includes office assistants, data entry operators, loaders (loading and unloading of goods), housekeeping staff, heavy and light vehicle drivers, car drivers, security personnel, and other support staff. Organizations across Tamil Nadu require dependable manpower to manage essential operational functions.

DHK FMS partners with such organizations by delivering efficient, compliant, and scalable manpower solutions tailored to their operational needs.

Our commitment, professionalism, and client-focused approach have enabled us to establish a strong presence in a short span of time. DHK FMS is duly registered under the State Labour Contract Act and holds valid ESI and PF registrations.

Vision

To become a market leader in manpower and facility management services by consistently enhancing client productivity and operational efficiency.

Mission

  • To deliver high-quality manpower services aligned with client requirements.
  • To deploy trained, reliable, and efficient personnel across all service categories.
  • To provide customized workforce solutions based on the unique needs of each client.
  • To follow a customer-centric approach with 24×7 support from planning to execution.

Our Strength

  • Supply of skilled and unskilled manpower tailored to business requirements
  • Enhancement of client productivity through efficient workforce deployment
  • Strict compliance with statutory labour laws and regulations
  • Maintenance of accurate and up-to-date employee records
  • Timely and transparent salary payments
  • Deployment of honest, disciplined, and professionally trained personnel

Our Clients

We proudly serve corporate offices, hospitals, hotels, IT parks, retail chains, warehouses, and industrial establishments across Tamil Nadu.
